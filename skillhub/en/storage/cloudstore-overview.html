The Secure Warehouse Analogy

Imagine your business generates mountains of documents, photos, videos, and data files every day. Where do you keep all of it?

Think of CloudStore as a secure, expandable warehouse for your digital assets. Just like a professional storage facility:

  • It grows with you — need more space? Simply request additional room. No construction required.
  • It's always accessible — authorised staff can retrieve items anytime, from anywhere, using a secure access pass.
  • It's professionally managed — the facility handles security, climate control, and maintenance. You focus on your business.
  • Your items are protected — multiple copies are stored in different locations, so even if one shelf is damaged, your belongings are safe.

CloudStore is OVHcloud's storage service within the OPCP platform. It lets you store, organise, and retrieve any type of digital data — from simple text files to large video archives — without managing physical hard drives or servers yourself.

How CloudStore Works

Common Use Cases

CloudStore is versatile enough to handle many different business needs. Here are the most common scenarios:

Document Storage

Store contracts, reports, spreadsheets, and presentations in a central location accessible to your entire team. Think of it as a shared filing cabinet that never runs out of drawers and can be accessed from any office.

Media Archives

Keep large collections of images, videos, and audio files safe and organised. Marketing teams, creative agencies, and media companies use CloudStore to maintain their growing libraries without worrying about running out of space.

Application Data

Software applications generate data constantly — user records, transaction logs, configuration files. CloudStore provides a reliable home for this data, ensuring applications can read and write information quickly and safely.

Backups

Create safety copies of your critical business data. If something goes wrong with your primary systems — hardware failure, accidental deletion, or a security incident — your backups in CloudStore let you recover quickly.

Why Customers Choose CloudStore

  • No hardware to manage — OVHcloud handles all physical infrastructure.
  • Pay for what you use — storage scales with your needs, no upfront investment in hard drives.
  • Data sovereignty — data stays in European data centres, meeting compliance requirements.
  • Built-in protection — automatic redundancy means your data is copied across multiple locations.
  • Access from anywhere — securely reach your data from any location with an internet connection.

Key Takeaways

  • CloudStore is OVHcloud's managed storage service — a secure, expandable warehouse for your digital assets.
  • It handles documents, media, application data, and backups without requiring you to manage physical hardware.
  • Data is automatically protected through redundancy and stored in European data centres.
  • You pay only for the storage you actually use, and can scale up or down as your business needs change.
