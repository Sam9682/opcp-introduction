What Makes OPCP Stand Out

OPCP (OVHcloud Hosted Private Cloud) offers a set of features designed to give businesses the power of dedicated infrastructure with the convenience of a managed service. Each feature below is explained with a visual diagram to help you understand how it works.

1. Dedicated Resources

Every node in your OPCP environment is exclusively yours. No other customer shares your CPU, RAM, or storage. Think of it like having your own private gym versus a public one — the equipment is always available when you need it, with no waiting.

2. On-Demand Scalability

Need more power? Add nodes. Need less? Remove them. OPCP lets you scale your infrastructure up or down based on business needs — like adding extra rooms to your office when the team grows, or releasing them when a project ends.

3. Network Isolation

Your private network is completely separated from other customers and the public internet. It's like having internal corridors in your office building that only your employees can access — visitors use the main entrance, but your internal communications stay private.

4. API-Driven Management

Everything you can do in the dashboard, you can also do through the API. This means repetitive tasks can be automated — like having a personal assistant who follows your instructions precisely, every time, without you needing to be present.

5. European Data Sovereignty

All data stays within European data centres, governed by European law. This is critical for businesses in regulated industries. Think of it like keeping your company's legal documents in a local solicitor's office rather than sending them overseas — you know exactly where they are and which laws protect them.
