Why Businesses Choose OPCP

OVHcloud's Hosted Private Cloud delivers real business value. Think of it like upgrading from renting a desk in a co-working space to having your own private office — same building, same amenities, but now you control who enters, how it's arranged, and when you use it.

Here are the key benefits that matter most to businesses:

1. Cost Predictability

The Benefit

With OPCP, you pay a fixed monthly fee for dedicated resources. No surprise bills, no usage spikes that blow your budget. It's like having a fixed-rate mortgage instead of a variable one — you always know what you'll pay.

Use Case: The Growing E-Commerce Company

Problem: A mid-size online retailer was using pay-as-you-go cloud services. During Black Friday sales, their computing costs tripled unexpectedly, eating into profit margins. Budget planning became impossible.

OPCP Solution: By moving to OPCP, they locked in a predictable monthly cost for dedicated resources that handle peak traffic. Their CFO can now forecast IT spending accurately, and seasonal spikes no longer cause budget surprises.

2. Enhanced Security and Compliance

The Benefit

Your data lives on hardware that nobody else can access. It's like having a private vault in a bank — the bank provides the security guards and alarm systems, but only you have the key to your vault. Data stays in European data centres, meeting GDPR and data sovereignty requirements.

Use Case: The Healthcare Provider

Problem: A healthcare network needed to store patient records digitally but faced strict regulations about data isolation and geographic location. Shared cloud environments couldn't guarantee that patient data wouldn't be co-located with other organisations' workloads.

OPCP Solution: OPCP provides physically isolated resources in certified European data centres. The healthcare provider meets all regulatory requirements while benefiting from OVHcloud's enterprise-grade security infrastructure, including 24/7 monitoring and regular security audits.

3. Guaranteed Performance

The Benefit

Because resources are dedicated exclusively to you, there's no "noisy neighbour" problem. Imagine living in a detached house versus a flat — in a flat, your neighbour's loud party affects you. With OPCP, your computing performance is never impacted by what other companies are doing.

Use Case: The Financial Services Firm

Problem: A trading platform experienced unpredictable latency spikes on shared infrastructure. During market opening hours, other tenants' workloads would compete for the same resources, causing their transaction processing to slow down at the worst possible time.

OPCP Solution: With dedicated resources on OPCP, the firm gets consistent, predictable performance regardless of what's happening elsewhere. Their transactions process at the same speed whether it's a quiet Tuesday afternoon or a volatile Monday morning.

4. Data Sovereignty

The Benefit

Your data stays in Europe, governed by European laws. Think of it like keeping your important documents in a local safe deposit box rather than shipping them overseas — you know exactly where they are and which laws protect them.

Use Case: The Government Contractor

Problem: A company bidding on government contracts needed to guarantee that sensitive project data would never leave European jurisdiction. Many major cloud providers route data through non-European data centres, creating compliance risks.

OPCP Solution: OVHcloud guarantees data residency in European data centres. The contractor can certify to government clients that all data remains under European legal jurisdiction, winning contracts that require strict data sovereignty.
