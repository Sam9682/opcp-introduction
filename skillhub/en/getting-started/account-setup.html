Account Setup

Beginner 🇫🇷 Français

Overview

Setting up your OPCP account is the first step to accessing OVHcloud's private cloud services. Think of it like opening a bank account: you provide your details, verify your identity, and then you receive access to your own secure space.

This guide walks you through each step of the process with clear visual references so you know exactly where to click.

What You Need Before Starting

  • A valid business email address
  • Your company name and registration details
  • A phone number for verification
  • A payment method (credit card or SEPA direct debit)

Step 1: Visit the OVHcloud Sign-Up Page

Open your web browser and go to the OVHcloud website. Look for the "Create an account" button in the top-right corner of the page.

Step 2: Choose Your Account Type

Select whether you are creating a personal or business account. For OPCP services, choose Business Account — this gives you access to enterprise features and dedicated support.

Step 3: Fill In Your Company Details

Enter your company information in the registration form. Required fields include:

  • Company name — Your official registered business name
  • Contact email — This becomes your login email
  • Phone number — Used for account verification and security alerts
  • Country — Determines your nearest datacentre options

Step 4: Verify Your Email

After submitting the form, OVHcloud sends a verification email to the address you provided. Open the email and click the confirmation link. This step proves you own the email address and activates your account.

Step 5: Set Up Your Password

Create a strong password for your account. OVHcloud requires passwords to be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Think of your password as the key to your private cloud — keep it secure and unique.

Step 6: Add a Payment Method

Finally, add a payment method to your account. This is required before you can order OPCP services. You can choose between a credit card or SEPA direct debit for recurring payments.

What Happens Next

Once your account is set up and verified, you can log in to the OVHcloud Manager and begin exploring OPCP services. Your next step is learning how to access the dashboard.
