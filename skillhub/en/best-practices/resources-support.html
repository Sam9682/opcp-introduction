Support Channels

When documentation is not enough, OVHcloud provides multiple ways to get direct help from support professionals.

Support Ticket System

Submit a support request through the OVHcloud Control Panel. Tickets are tracked, prioritised based on severity, and handled by specialised teams. Best for non-urgent issues, detailed technical questions, or when you need a written record of the resolution.

Open a Ticket →

Phone Support

Speak directly with a support agent for urgent issues or when you need real-time guidance. Phone support is available for customers with Premium or Business support plans. Check your contract for availability hours and contact numbers.

View Support Levels →

Live Chat

Available within the OVHcloud Control Panel for quick questions and guidance. Chat agents can help with account issues, billing queries, and point you to the right documentation for technical questions.

Start Chat →

Tips for Getting Help Effectively

  • Search documentation first — Many questions are already answered in the guides and tutorials.
  • Be specific — When contacting support, include what you were doing, what you expected, and what actually happened.
  • Include identifiers — Provide your project ID, service name, or ticket number to speed up resolution.
  • Check the status page — Before opening a ticket, verify there is no ongoing incident affecting your region.
  • Use the community — Other users may have encountered and solved the same issue.
